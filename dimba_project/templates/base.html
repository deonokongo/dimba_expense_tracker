Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about tracking your expenses? Check out the answers to the most commonly asked questions below.

How do I start tracking my expenses?

Simply sign up, add your expense categories, and start recording your daily expenses. Our platform will help you categorize and analyze them for easy tracking.

Can I track both personal and business expenses?

Yes, you can create separate categories for personal and business expenses to keep them organized and distinct.

Will I be able to generate reports?

Yes, our platform allows you to generate detailed reports on your expenses, helping you monitor spending trends and make informed financial decisions.

What is an expense tracker and how does it work?

An expense tracker is a tool designed to help individuals or businesses monitor and manage their spending. It works by categorizing expenses and providing reports to help users track their financial habits, budget efficiently, and stay on top of their financial goals.

How can I categorize my expenses?

Expenses can be categorized into various groups like groceries, transportation, utilities, entertainment, and more. By assigning categories to each expense, users can track their spending in a more organized manner and gain insights into their spending patterns.

Can I track both personal and business expenses with the tracker?

Yes, our expense tracker allows you to manage both personal and business expenses separately. You can create different categories or profiles for personal and business expenses to keep them organized and distinct, making it easy to track both aspects of your finances.
